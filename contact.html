Quick Answers

Frequently asked

How long does shipping take?

Most orders are dispatched within 24-48 hours. Delivery typically takes 3-7 business days depending on location. COD available across India.

What's your return policy?

Seven-day easy return on all pieces. Item must be in original condition with tags. We arrange free reverse pickup in most cities.

Are your products real gold?

We craft premium imitation jewellery — gold-tone brass alloy with anti-tarnish coating. Looks identical to fine gold, at a fraction of the cost.

How do I shop during live salons?

Join the YouTube live, type the piece number in chat to reserve. We DM you on WhatsApp to confirm address and details.

Do you offer Cash on Delivery?

Yes. Cash on Delivery available across India. A small ₹50 COD charge applies. Pay only when you receive your jewellery.
