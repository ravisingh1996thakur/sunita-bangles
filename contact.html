Frequently Asked Questions

📦 How long does shipping take?

Most orders are dispatched within 24-48 hours. Delivery typically takes 3-7 business days depending on location. COD available all India.

7-day easy return on all products. The item must be in original condition with tags. We arrange free reverse pickup in most cities.

We sell premium imitation jewellery — gold-tone brass alloy with anti-tarnish coating. Looks identical to real gold at a fraction of the price.
