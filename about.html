The Atelier

Home · The Atelier
Founded with love · Est. 2018

A family workshop, quietly becoming a beloved house

Sunita Bangles began in a modest workshop, with a single belief — that every Indian woman deserves jewellery that feels precious, regardless of budget. What started as a passion has become a quiet movement.

We aren't a faceless brand. We're a family. Sunita Ji personally curates every piece, answers your WhatsApp, hosts you live every week. We treat every customer as if they've walked into our home.

Today, our atelier serves over ten thousand families across India — each carrying a piece of our story with them.
