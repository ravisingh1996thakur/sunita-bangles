From a Family Workshop to 10,000+ Homes Across India

Sunita Bangles started in a small workshop with one simple promise — bring premium-looking jewellery to every woman, at honest prices, with the warmth of family.

What began as a passion project run by Sunita Ji has grown into a beloved brand. Every necklace, every bangle, every earring is personally curated by her — bringing decades of jewellery expertise into pieces that feel timeless yet modern.

We aren't a faceless e-commerce brand. We're a real family who answers your WhatsApp, hosts you live every week, and treats every customer like they're walking into our home.
